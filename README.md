BACKGROUND 
The problem is that the majority of people on this planet don’t really take care of the environment, waste resources like water and electricity, and they often throw trash anywhere, disregarding the importance of keeping their surroundings green and clean. As a result, our planet is suffering from challenges like climate change, pollution, and loss of trees, which affect everyone. 
 
OBJECTIVES 
To help everyone solve this problem, we decided to create an app that encourages people to take care of the environment in a fun way. In this app, users have to grow a tree every day, which reminds them to take actions like reducing waste, planting trees, and saving energy. Most importantly, this can help people build everyday habits that can protect our nature at the same time, also making them more aware of how their small actions can make a big difference for the environment. 
 
METHODOLOGY APPROACH 
We aim to make a game that targets people who have trouble in maintaining healthy plants, through multiple gameplay features like the growing and withering of a plant, as well as exchanging unhealthy soil for healthy soil, and many more real-life techniques. 
 
UNIQUENESS  
Our game heavily takes inspiration from the Japanese toys called Tamagotchis. Although our main idea of the game is very similar, our gameplay is completely different. Unlike other games, we have an “offline” feature where the game updates without the player having to launch the game.
